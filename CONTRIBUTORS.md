# Contributors

Active contributors listed here receive a contributor commercial license
as described in the [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md) (Part 3).

## How It Works

- **Active contributors** get a free non-competing commercial license for
  their current employer/organization
- The license covers the organization listed at the time of addition
- Transfer to a different organization requires explicit approval from
  the copyright holder
- Contributors moved to the historical list retain license rights for
  6 months after removal
- See [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md) Part 3 for full terms

## How to Become a Contributor

1. Make meaningful contributions to the project (code, docs, testing, etc.)
2. Open a PR or contact the maintainer
3. Upon acceptance, you'll be added to the active list with your organization
